FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

🚚 SHIPPING
How long does delivery take? ＋
Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days nationwide. Metro Manila may receive orders faster (1–3 days). Express options are available at checkout for 1–2 day delivery.
Is free shipping available? ＋
Yes! Free standard shipping on all orders ₱500 and above. Orders below ₱500 have a flat ₱99 shipping fee. Use coupon FREESHIP for free shipping on any order amount.
Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)? ＋
Yes! COD is available nationwide. You can also pay via GCash or Maya at checkout.
🔄 RETURNS
What is your return policy? ＋
We accept returns within 15 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging. To initiate a return, go to My Orders, select the order, and click "Request Return."
What if I receive a damaged or wrong item? ＋
Take photos of the item and contact us within 3 days of delivery. We'll arrange a free return and send a replacement or full refund.
How long do refunds take? ＋
Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days after the returned item is received and inspected.
👤 ACCOUNT
Do I need an account to order? ＋
Yes, a free account is required to place orders. This lets us track your orders and save your shipping addresses.
How do I track my order? ＋
Go to My Profile → My Orders to see real-time status updates for all your orders.
🎟️ COUPONS
How do I use a coupon? ＋
Add items to your cart, then enter the coupon code in the cart page before checkout. Active coupons are listed on the Coupons page.
Can I use multiple coupons? ＋
Only one coupon can be applied per order. Choose the one that gives you the best discount!
