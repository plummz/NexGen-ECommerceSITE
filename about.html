🏪 OUR STORY

John Rey's NexGen Shop started as a small passion project — a love for Pokémon collectibles and tech gadgets that turned into a full-fledged online store. What began with just a handful of Pokémon figures has grown into a one-stop-shop serving thousands of Filipino customers nationwide.

We believe that every Filipino deserves access to quality products at fair prices — from the latest smartphones and laptops, to rare Pokémon figures, stylish fashion, healthy skincare, and even your favorite snacks. No matter what you're looking for, NexGen has it.

Based in the Philippines and proud of it! We ship to every corner of the archipelago — from Metro Manila to the most remote islands. Our goal is simple: happy customers, quality products, and fast delivery.
