📦 ORDERS
How do I place an order? ▼
Browse our products, select your preferred variation, add to cart, then proceed to checkout. Fill in your delivery details and choose your payment method. Once submitted, you'll see an order confirmation with your Order ID.
Can I cancel or modify my order? ▼
You can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placing it. After that, orders are sent to processing. Contact us immediately at 09671756325 via call or SMS to request a cancellation.
How do I track my order? ▼
Once your order is shipped, we'll send a tracking number via SMS to the phone number you provided at checkout. You can also view your order status in My Profile → My Orders.
What happens if I'm not home during delivery? ▼
Our courier will attempt delivery up to 2 times. If nobody is available, they'll leave a notice and your package will be held at the nearest hub for 3–5 days. Contact us or the courier to arrange re-delivery or pickup.
Can I order in bulk or wholesale? ▼
Yes! For bulk orders of 10+ units or wholesale inquiries, contact us at 09671756325. We offer special discounts for bulk purchases and resellers.
Do you have a minimum order amount? ▼
No minimum order! However, free shipping applies to orders ₱500 and above. Orders below ₱500 have an ₱80 shipping fee.
What is my order ID for? ▼
Your Order ID (e.g. NXG-ABC123) is your unique reference number. Use it when contacting us about a specific order, tracking deliveries, or requesting refunds.
Can I pre-order items that are out of stock? ▼
Yes! Contact us at 09671756325 to inquire about pre-orders. We'll notify you once the item is available and hold your reservation.
🚚 SHIPPING & DELIVERY
How long does delivery take? ▼
Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days depending on your location:
  • Metro Manila: 1–3 days
  • Major Cities (Cebu, Davao, Iloilo): 3–5 days
  • Provincial Areas: 5–7 days
  • Remote Islands: 7–10 days
How much is the shipping fee? ▼
Shipping is FREE for all orders ₱500 and above! For orders below ₱500, a flat shipping fee of ₱80 applies. You can also use the coupon code FREESHIP for free shipping on any order.
Do you ship nationwide? ▼
Yes! We ship to all provinces and cities across the Philippines including Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. Remote island areas may take longer and may have additional fees.
What courier do you use? ▼
We use trusted couriers including J&T Express, LBC, Grab Express, and Lalamove depending on your area. You'll receive your tracking number via SMS once shipped.
Do you offer same-day or next-day delivery? ▼
Same-day delivery is available in select Metro Manila areas for an additional fee. Contact us at 09671756325 before placing your order to check availability.
What if my order arrives late? ▼
If your order arrives later than the estimated delivery date, you are entitled to ₱50 compensation. Contact us at 09671756325 with your Order ID to claim this.
🔄 RETURNS & REFUNDS
What is your return policy? ▼
We offer a 15-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and with all accessories/tags intact. Items damaged due to misuse are not eligible for return.
My item arrived damaged. What do I do? ▼
Take a photo or video of the damaged item and contact us within 3 days of delivery at 09671756325. We'll arrange a free replacement or full refund immediately — no questions asked.
How do refunds work? ▼
Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. Refunds are sent via GCash, Maya, or bank transfer depending on your original payment method. COD refunds are sent via GCash.
I received the wrong item. What now? ▼
We sincerely apologize! Contact us at 09671756325 with your Order ID and a photo of the item received. We'll ship the correct item at no additional cost and arrange a return for the wrong item.
Who shoulders the return shipping cost? ▼
For damaged, defective, or wrong items — we cover all return shipping costs. For buyer's remorse or change of mind returns, the buyer shoulders the return shipping fee.

👤 ACCOUNT & PROFILE
Do I need an account to order? ▼
You can browse and add to cart without an account, but you'll need to create one to complete checkout and track your orders. Registration is free and only takes 30 seconds!
Will my account and cart be saved after closing the browser? ▼
Yes! Your account, cart, wishlist, and orders are all saved permanently in your browser's local storage. They will remain even after closing the browser or restarting your computer.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? ▼
Go to My Profile → Security. Enter your current password (if you remember it) and set a new one. If you've completely forgotten your password, contact us at 09671756325 and we'll help you manually.
How do I update my delivery address? ▼
Go to My Profile → My Address and update your address. This will be pre-filled next time you check out. You can also update it directly during checkout.
How do I delete my account? ▼
Contact us at 09671756325 and request account deletion. We'll process it within 24 hours. Note that your order history will also be permanently deleted.

🎟️ COUPONS & DISCOUNTS
How do I use a coupon code? ▼
Add items to your cart, then enter your coupon code in the "Coupon Code" box in your cart and click Apply. The discount will be deducted from your total automatically.
Where can I find available coupon codes? ▼
Visit our Coupons page to see all available codes. We also announce new promos via SMS — make sure your phone number is saved in your profile!
Can I use multiple coupons in one order? ▼
Only one coupon code can be applied per order. Choose the one that gives you the biggest discount for your specific order.
My coupon code is not working. What should I do? ▼
